// Who We Are

About SGIPC

A programming-focused student club at KUET — built by programmers, for programmers.

Special Groups Interested
In Programming Contest

SGIPC is the official competitive programming club of the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET). We are a community of passionate programmers united by one goal — to compete, improve, and represent KUET at the highest level.

From solving your first problem on Codeforces to standing at the ICPC World Finals — SGIPC is with you every step of the way. We believe that consistent practice, the right guidance, and a strong team culture can take any student from beginner to world-class.

⚔️
Competitive Programming
Regular problem solving sessions, contest participation, and algorithm training from basics to advanced.
🏆
ICPC / IUPC Preparation
Dedicated training for regional and national contests. Structured team formation and mock contests before every major event.
👥
Team Practice & Culture
Weekly team practices, peer learning, and a competitive environment that pushes everyone to be their best.
🧠
Beginner to Advanced Roadmap
From your first "Hello World" to advanced DS&A — SGIPC provides a clear, structured path to improvement.
